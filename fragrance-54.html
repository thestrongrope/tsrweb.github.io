FRAGRANCE OF MASTERSHIP - TRADITION: 54

Prophets and angels request Allah to visit Imam Husain (peace be upon him) in Karbala

اَبِي عَبْدِ اللهِ ع قَالَ ...لَيْسَ مِنْ مَلَكٍ مُقَرَّبٍ وَ لَا نَبِيٍّ مُرْسَلٍ اِلَّا وَ هُوَ يَسْاَلُ اللهَ اَنْ يَزُورَهُ‏ فَفَوْجٌ‏ يَهْبِطُ وَ فَوْجٌ يَصْعَدُ.

Imam Sadeq (peace be upon him) says,

“...thus, there is neither a proximate angel nor a sent Prophet but that he requests Allah to visit him (al-Husain). So, a group descends (for ziyaarat) and another ascends (back to the skies).”

References:

1.    Kaamil al-Ziyaaraat, p. 112, H. 3

2.    Madinatul Ma’ajiz, vol. 4, p. 204, H. 1225

3.    Behaar al-Anwaar, vol. 98, p. 60, H. 33
