FRAGRANCE OF MASTERSHIP - TRADITION: 41

The Naasebi is the one who hates the lovers of the Divinely Appointed Hujjat

عَنْ اَبِي عَبْدِ اللهِ ع قَالَ‏ لَيْسَ النَّاصِبُ مَنْ نَصَبَ لَنَا اَهْلَ الْبَيْتِ لِاَنَّكَ لَا تَجِدُ رَجُلًا يَقُولُ‏ اَنَا أُبْغِضُ‏ مُحَمَّدًا وَ آلَ‏ مُحَمَّدٍ وَ لَكِنَّ النَّاصِبَ مَنْ نَصَبَ لَكُمْ وَ هُوَ يَعْلَمُ اَنَّكُمْ تَتَوَلَّوْنَّا وَ اَنَّكُمْ مِنْ شِيعَتِنَا

Imam Sadeq (peace be upon him) said,

“He is not a Naasebi who bears enmity against us Ahle Bait (peace be upon him). For, you will certainly not find anyone say, ‘I hate Muhammad and the progeny of Muhammad (peace be upon him and his holy progeny)’ but a Naasebi is the one who bears enmity against you (Shiahs) knowing that you have accepted our mastership and that you are from our Shiahs.”

References:

1.    Maani al-Akhbaar, p. 365, H. 1

2.    Elal al-Sharaae, vol. 2, p. 601

3.    Wasaael al-Shiah, vol. 9, p. 486, H. 12548

4.    Behaar al-Anwaar, vol. 8, p. 369
