FRAGRANCE OF MASTERSHIP - TRADITION: 212

The Messenger of Allah (peace be upon him and his holy progeny) will compensate the one who helps the Ahle Bait (peace be upon them)

عَنْ اَمِيرِ الْمُؤْمِنِينَؑ قَالَ قَالَ رَسُولُ اللهِ ﷺ مَنِ اصْطَنَعَ اِلَى اَحَدٍ مِنْ اَهْلِ بَيْتِي يَداً كَافَيْتُهُ يَوْمَ الْقِيَامَةِ 

Ameer al-Momineen Ali Ibn Abi Talib (peace be upon him) reports that the Messenger of Allah (peace be upon him and his holy progeny) said,

“Whoever helps anyone (of the Imams) from my Ahle Bait (peace be upon them), I will compensate him on the Day of Judgment.”

Notes:

1)    The Messenger of Allah (peace be upon him and his holy progeny) has undertaken to compensate the one who helps any Imam (peace be upon him) from his Ahle Bait (peace be upon them). It is beyond one’s imagination to visualize the quantity and quality of this reward. 

2)    Every follower of Ahle Bait (peace be upon them) must strive to devote some part of his week – if not the day – to serve the Imams of the Ahle Bait (peace be upon them), especially the Imam of his time (a.t.f.s.)

References:

1.    Al-Mahaasin, vol. 1, p. 63, H. 111, Chapter 87

2.    Tafseer al-Furaat, p. 311

3.    Sa’d al-So’ood, p. 257

4.    Behaar al-Anwaar, vol. 26, p. 228, H. 6
