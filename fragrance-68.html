FRAGRANCE OF MASTERSHIP - TRADITION: 68

Divine Imams (peace be upon them) – The uninterrupted words of Allah

عَنْ عَبْدِ اللهِ بْنِ جُنْدَبٍ قَالَ سَاَلْتُ اَبَا الْحَسَنِؑ عَنْ قَوْلِ اللهِ عَزَّ وَ جَلَّ وَ لَقَدْ وَصَّلْنا لَهُمُ الْقَوْلَ لَعَلَّهُمْ يَتَذَكَّرُونَ قَالَ اِمَامٌ اِلَى اِمَامٍ

Abdullah Ibn Jundab reports, “I asked Imam Kazem (peace be upon him) about the word of Allah – Mighty and Majestic be He - “And indeed We have joined the ‘word’ uninterrupted perhaps they may be mindful[1]”. He (peace be upon him) said, “One Imam to (another) Imam.”

References:

1.    Kaafi, vol. 1, p. 415, H. 18 بَابٌ فِيهِ نُكَتٌ وَ نُتَفٌ مِنَ التَّنْزِيلِ فِي الْوَلَايَةِ

[1]        Surah Qasas (28): Verse 51
