FRAGRANCE OF MASTERSHIP - TRADITION: 242

Who claims to love the Holy Prophet (peace be on him and his progeny) but does not love Ali (peace be on him) is a liar

قَالَ النَّبِيُّ ﷺ وَ اَخَذَ بِيَدِ عَلِيٍّ ؑ مَنْ زَعَمَ اَنَّهٗ يُحِبُّنِي وَ لَا يُحِبُّ هٰذَا فَقَدْ كَذَبَ

The Holy Prophet (peace be on him and his progeny) took the hand of Ali (peace be on him) and declared,

“Whoever thinks that he loves me but does not love him (viz. Ali) then indeed he has lied.”

References:

1.    Oyoon-o-Akhbaar al-Reza (peace be on him), vol. 2, p. 60, H. 231, Chapter 31

2.    Behaar al-Anwaar, vol. 27, p. 79, H. 17
